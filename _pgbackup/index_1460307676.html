A little About Me

Hello. My name is Chris Kibble, and I'm an Air Force officer stationed at the Pentagon. I spent many years flying all over the world. But my current job is to ensure the Air Force has enough fighter & bomber pilots to meet its mission.

My military service is quickly coming to an end and I'll be transitioning back to civilian life in October. And since I've always loved technology, I've decided to pursue a full-time tech career.

In order to ease my upcoming tech transition, I've spent the last two years learning to code on nights and weekends. But unfortunately I've never been able to pursue coding full time until now.

That's what lead me to General Assembly and your full-time development courses. I'm looking forward to being able to commit full-time to a structured coding curriculum. And get real-time feedback from experienced instructors. I'm confident that with work and dedication I can take my skills to new heights!
